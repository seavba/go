Welcome to Articles API

With this API you can get information from our Articles, post a new article and delete them. Use the following commands from your terminal in order to interact with our API.

        
    
          
  1. Get all the articles
    2. 
            curl -s https://localhost/articles | jq .
    
  [{"Id":"1","Title":"Hello","desc":"Article Description","content":"Article Content"},{"Id":"2","Title":"Hello 2","desc":"Article Description","content":"Article Content"}]    
          
  2. Get a single article
    3. 
            curl -s https://localhost/article/1 | jq .
    
  {"Id":"1","Title":"Hello","desc":"Article Description","content":"Article Content"}    
          
  3. Add an article
    4. 
            curl -d '{"Id": "3", "Title": "My new product", "Desc": "New Article Description", "Content": "New Article Content"}' -H "Content-Type: application/json" -X POST https://localhost/article
    
  {"Id":"3","Title":"My new product","desc":"New Article Description","content":"New Article Content"}    
          
  4. Delete an article
    5. 
            curl -X DELETE https://localhost/article/3
